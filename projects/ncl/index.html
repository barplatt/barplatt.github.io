Flooring

About Us

At a time when major corporations jostling to position themselves in the high ticket market with gimmicks and expensive ads, NewCastle Living is focusing on offering high quality flooring products at exceptionally low prices. We achieve this by emphasizing reliable and quality-centric sourcing combined with old fashion business practices such as letting our products and services to speak for themselves.

As a major distributor of laminate and engineered flooring, we offer exclusive product lines that surpass any expectation. Our large network of suppliers is a product of years of research and thousands of miles of travel to conduct background examination and onsite evaluation. This network of reliable suppliers combined with years of experience in logistical tasks have enabled us to bring flooring products with substantial warranty – some as much as life time.

Aside from focusing on quality and affordability, NewCastle Living places great emphasis on eco-friendly products that adhere to strict standards. To emphasis our commitment to green products, we now offer North American Naturals, our laminate flooring constructed based on recycle material that contributes to credits with green building systems like LEED.

To simply summarize the company in terms of high quality flooring, marked as green, at affordable prices is only a partial indicative of our mission and identity. At NewCastle Living we are inspired to offer products that would transform an ordinary home into a castle or your dream.
